## Boundary

- Keep operational methods, templates, and sanitized lessons here.
- Do not store raw runtime state, real hostnames, private paths, tokens, account secrets, exact schedules, or unredacted command output.
- Promote reusable repeatable workflows into `kt-aicoding/agent-workflows` or `kt-aicoding/skills` when they become agent-executable procedures.
